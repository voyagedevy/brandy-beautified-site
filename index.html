What is Daxxify?

Daxxify is a peptide-powered neurotoxin used to soften movement-related lines. Brandy uses it strategically so the result looks refreshed, not frozen.

How long can Daxxify last?

Longevity varies by client, dose, area, and metabolism, but many clients choose Daxxify because it may last longer than traditional tox options.
